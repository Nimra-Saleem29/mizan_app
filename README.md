## 📱 AI-Powered Legal Assistant for Pakistani Citizens

MIZAN is an AI-driven mobile application designed to make **legal information accessible, understandable, and free for every Pakistani citizen**.

It transforms complex legal language into **simple Urdu, English, and Roman Urdu explanations**, helping users understand their rights instantly through AI, OCR, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Pakistan’s legal system is difficult for ordinary citizens to understand due to:

- Complex legal English
- Lack of accessible legal tools
- Expensive legal consultations
- No simplified legal awareness systems

As a result, citizens often:
- Misunderstand their rights
- Rely on incorrect advice
- Avoid legal action entirely

👉 MIZAN solves this gap by making law **simple, searchable, and accessible in your language**.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

MIZAN is a **mobile AI legal assistant** powered by:

- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Fine-tuned NLP models
- Urdu + English multilingual support
- Legal document intelligence

Users can:
- Ask legal questions in Urdu/English/Roman Urdu
- Upload FIRs or legal documents
- Speak using voice input
- Receive accurate, cited legal explanations

---

## ⚙️ Core Features

### ⚖️ 1. Bilingual Legal Q&A Engine
- Ask legal questions in Urdu, English, or Roman Urdu
- AI retrieves real Pakistani legal documents
- Generates **plain-language answers with citations**
- Prevents hallucination using RAG

---

### 🚨 2. FIR Analyzer
- Upload FIR images or text
- Extracts PPC sections automatically
- Explains charges in simple Urdu
- Shows punishment, bail status, and jurisdiction
- Detects procedural errors

---

### 📄 3. Document Scanner & Legal Analyzer
- Scan rent agreements, contracts, notices, etc.
- Extract key legal clauses using OCR + NLP
- Highlights risks and unfair terms
- Explains rights in simple language

---

### 🎙️ 4. Urdu Voice Input
- Speak your legal question
- Supports Urdu + Roman Urdu accents
- Converts speech to text using Whisper
- Provides audio answers in Urdu

---

### 🧭 5. Know Your Rights (Scenario Engine)
Guided legal help for real-life situations:
- Arrest scenarios
- Salary disputes
- Evictions
- Court notices
- Divorce / Khula
- Road accidents

---

## 🧠 AI & Machine Learning Stack

- 🔹 RAG Pipeline (FAISS + LangChain)
- 🔹 BERT / DistilBERT (legal classification)
- 🔹 Mistral-7B / Gemini (text generation)
- 🔹 Whisper (speech-to-text)
- 🔹 Tesseract OCR (document scanning)
- 🔹 spaCy NER (entity extraction)
- 🔹 scikit-learn (prediction models)
- 🔹 mT5 / mBART (Urdu-English translation)
